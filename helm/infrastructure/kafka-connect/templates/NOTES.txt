Troubleshooting:

    kubectl get kafkaconnector s3-sink-raw -n kafka -o yaml

  Check pod events:
    kubectl describe pod kafka-connect-cluster-connect-0 -n kafka

  Search for errors:
    kubectl logs -n kafka kafka-connect-cluster-connect-0 --tail=100 | grep -iE "error|exception"
